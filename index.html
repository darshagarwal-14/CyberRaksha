About Us

Empowering digital safety for everyone.

Cyber Raksha is a student‑driven cybersecurity initiative on a mission to empower India’s youth—and the communities that support them—as proactive digital defenders. Founded in 2025, our network spans ten Indian states—Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Delhi, Maharashtra, Karnataka and West Bengal—and brings together 50+ dedicated student ambassadors alongside teachers and cybersecurity professionals.

Through hands‑on workshops in partner schools, engaging social‑media campaigns and a podcast series, we demystify core topics such as digital hygiene, threat detection, secure coding and privacy best practices. Our student‑led hack‑incubators guide teams to design, develop and publicly release open‑source security tools—accompanied by clear, step‑by‑step tutorials—so that anyone can adopt stronger defenses.
